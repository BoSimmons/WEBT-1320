School of Fire

Overview: The Fire School specializes in powerful offensive spells that inflict damage over time. Students of the Fire School are known for their aggressive approach, using blazing flames to overwhelm their enemies.

  • Primary Spells: Fireball, Meteor Strike, Inferno
  • Specialty: High damage output with the ability to cause "dot" (damage over time) effects.
  • Key Character Traits: Bold, relentless, and passionate.

School of Ice

Overview: The Ice School focuses on defense and crowd control. Ice wizards are adept at shielding themselves and their allies while slowing down opponents with icy spells.

  • Primary Spells: Ice Beam, Snowball, Frostbite
  • Specialty: High health and resistances with spells that can freeze or slow enemies.
  • Key Character Traits: Strategic, resilient, and protective.

School of Storm

Overview: The Storm School harnesses the raw power of thunderstorms and lightning. Storm wizards excel at delivering high damage in a short burst, though they are generally less resilient to attacks.

  • Primary Spells: Thunderstorm, Lightning Strike, Storm Lord
  • Specialty: Massive burst damage with a focus on elemental strikes.
  • Key Character Traits: Bold, high-risk/high-reward, and energetic.

School of Myth

Overview: The Myth School delves into the realm of legends and creatures. Myth wizards summon mythical beasts to aid them in battle and cast spells that can alter the course of combat.

  • Primary Spells: Troll, Basilisk, Minotaur
  • Specialty: Summoning creatures and casting spells with a focus on versatility and utility.
  • Key Character Traits: Imaginative, resourceful, and strategic.

School of Life

Overview: The Life School is dedicated to healing and support. Life wizards use their magic to restore health to themselves and their allies, making them invaluable in both solo and group play.

  • Primary Spells: Healing Light, Satyr, Unicorn
  • Specialty: Restoration of health and boosting the well-being of allies.
  • Key Character Traits: Compassionate, supportive, and nurturing.

School of Death

Overview: The Death School focuses on draining life from enemies and converting it into health for the wizard. Death wizards are known for their ability to weaken foes while strengthening themselves.

  • Primary Spells: Scorpion, Vampire, Grim Harvest
  • Specialty: Life-stealing spells and converting damage into health.
  • Key Character Traits: Mysterious, tactical, and resilient.

School of Balance

Overview: The Balance School excels in providing versatile support spells and balanced damage. Balance wizards are known for their adaptability, using their spells to support allies and weaken enemies.

  • Primary Spells: Sandstorm, Judgement, Balance Blade
  • Specialty: Versatile spells that balance offensive and defensive capabilities.
  • Key Character Traits: Adaptable, fair-minded, and strategic.
