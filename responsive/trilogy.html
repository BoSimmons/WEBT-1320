Kingsisle Entertainment

KingsIsle Entertainment is a prominent video game developer and publisher based in Austin, Texas, best known for its creation of the popular online games "Wizard101" and "Pirate101." Founded in 2005, KingsIsle has carved out a niche in the gaming industry by focusing on family-friendly, massively multiplayer online role-playing games (MMORPGs) that offer engaging worlds, rich storytelling, and a strong sense of community. With a commitment to creating immersive and accessible experiences, KingsIsle has built a dedicated player base and continues to be a notable player in the online gaming space

Ravenwood Academy: A Wizard101 Story

a captivating expansion of the beloved world of Wizard101, immersing players deeper into the enchanting universe of the game. This narrative-driven adventure introduces players to Ravenwood Academy, the prestigious school where young wizards train to master their magical skills. As players explore the academy’s hallowed halls and magical classrooms, they uncover hidden secrets and face new challenges that test their abilities and courage. Rich with lore, engaging characters, and intriguing quests, Ravenwood Academy: A Wizard101 Story offers a fresh perspective on the magical world of Wizard City and invites both new and seasoned players to experience the wonder and excitement of life as a student of magic.
