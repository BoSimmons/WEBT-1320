summary plot

Gravity-Falls is a Disney Channel show following two young twins to get sent by their parents to go visit their uncle for the summer break. the show shows the crazy adventures the twins go on, finding out about the hidden secrets of the small town and their uncle.

beginning

in the beginning of the show, one of the twins finds an old, handmade journal with an unknown author that writes about all of the secrets of the town, causing the twins to go and find things mentioned in the book. through this, they find many supernatural beings, some friendly, some not so much.
